Phil Grundy Insurance

Phil Grundy Insurance (PGI) has been serving businesses and individuals in Kentucky since 2006. We are a trusted broker, helping groups of all sizes with health, disability, and life insurance. Additionally, we assist individuals in finding coverage that best fits their unique needs.

Group Insurance: Health, disability, and life insurance for businesses ranging from 2- 1,000 employees.

Individual Insurance: Helping individuals find the best coverage for their needs. We can assist you in finding the best health, dental, vision and/or life insurance policies
Carriers You Work With: Anthem, United Health Care, Delta Dental, Aflac, Colonial Life, Cincinnati Life, etc.

For groups of 10-99 workers that do not qualify for group insurance we offer Kynect Qualified Health Plans
